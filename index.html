REPEATED DECISIONS / ENGINEERING POSITION

The work keeps returning to three boundaries.

MODEL / CODE

Judgment lives in models. Invariants live in code.

Let models interpret and propose. Let typed contracts, state machines, and deterministic guards decide what becomes real.

CLAIM / TRACE

Evidence before confidence.

Repeated runs, negative controls, replayable logs, and explicit limitations turn an interesting result into an engineering claim.

COMPLEX / LEGIBLE

Complexity needs an interface.

A difficult workflow becomes useful when people can inspect, edit, recover, and understand the state they are changing.
