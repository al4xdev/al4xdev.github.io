# Mentor content contract

The two mentor profiles are shared by the portfolio hero and the print-only A4 CV. Update only
`content.js` when replacing the mocks. Preserve both entries and both locales.

- `name`: public display name.
- `note`: one short line describing the mentorship relationship.
- `linkedin`: the mentor's complete public LinkedIn URL using HTTPS.
- `photo`: optional repository-relative image URL; leave blank to keep the initials placeholder.
- `initials`: two-character fallback used when `photo` is blank.

Do not invent private details, testimonials, job titles, or a relationship the mentor has not
approved for public display.
